The 15th Moment

Locking it up

Hunter used a bit of tomfoolery to trick Nathan in to thinking he had a boring night at a work dinner ahead. However, Nathan immediately knew that something was up when the black town car that came to pick him up and was sans Hunter. Inside he found fourteen envelopes, each highlighting a special moment in their relationship. The directions said when you get to #15, come and find Hunter.

At home, Hunter patiently waited (some say patiently, others say manically) while he decorated their apartment. Even after his 45-minute delay in Lake Shore Drive traffic during rush hour, Nathan needed a bit of coaching to come inside the apartment.

Cameras flashing, music playing, cake patiently waiting to be eaten and champagne on ice, Nathan said YES (#15, the moment Hunter promised forever) and their destiny to forever enjoy free 7/11 Slurpee’s on their engage-iversary was sealed.

After a surprise dinner at one of Nathan’s favorite spots with some of our closest friends, the fun continued all weekend with an engage-moon trip to New Buffalo, MI where even more of Nathan’s childhood best friends joined in on the fun.
